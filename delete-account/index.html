Car9t Account & Data Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

Car9t respects your privacy. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal information at any time.

This page explains how users of the Car9t mobile application can request account deletion.

How To Delete Your Account

To delete your Car9t account:

  • Send an email from your registered mobile number/email.
  • Mention "Delete My Car9t Account" in the subject.
  • Our support team will verify and process your request.

Email us:

support@car9t.com

Information Deleted

After account deletion, we remove:

  • User profile information
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Saved addresses
  • Vehicle information
  • Location information
  • Notification tokens

Information We May Retain

Some information may be retained when required for legal, security, tax, fraud prevention, or business record purposes.

Examples:

  • Payment transaction records
  • Invoices
  • Completed service history

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are usually completed within 7 business days after verification.
